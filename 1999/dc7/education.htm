DC-Education AGENDA for meeting at DC-7
From: Jon Mason & Stuart A. Sutton

DC-Eucation Charge

“The objectives of this Working Group are to discuss and develop a proposal for the use of Dublin Core metadata in the description of educational resources. The scope includes educational resources applicable for many national education communities and cross-sectoral communities (eg, K-12, Further and Higher Education, and Lifelong Learning).”

“The WG will develop qualifiers and/or extensions to the Dublin Core element set that will describe educational material for the purpose of enhancing resource discovery. It is expected that the resulting metadata will include educational qualifiers that fall within the scope of existing Dublin Core elements and potentially some that are specific to the domain of education.”
