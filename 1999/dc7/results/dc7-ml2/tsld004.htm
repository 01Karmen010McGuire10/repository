Basic principles (position paper)

  • Semantics of DC elements expressible in any human language

  • Working language of DC community is English

  • DC-Multilingual shares machine-readable tokens that stand for shared semantics

  • Proliferation of incompatible qualifiers threatens compatibility internationally

  • “We need to develop an RDF schema registry, starting with 15 elements in multiple languages”
