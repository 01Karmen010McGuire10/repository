DC-ML milestones

  • Founded at DC4 (Canberra), March 1997

  • May-November 1998: meetings in Bonn, Bangkok, Heraklion, Washington (DC-6) and position paper

  • October 1998: Tom Baker and Xu Bo (AIT) create simple registry prototype in 10 languages

  • April 1999: Shigeo Sugimoto and Mitsuharu Nagamori (ULIS) reimplement, improve code

  • October 1999: RDF schema near W3C approval; Eric Miller implements tools
