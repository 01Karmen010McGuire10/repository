NISO (1)

  • NISO has begun the activity to approve Dublin Core 1.1 as an American National Standard.

  • The effort to begin the standardization process for the Dublin Core was balloted and approved by the Voting Membership.

  • However NISO, like CEN, decided to wait for the formalization of DC 1.1 before proceding.
