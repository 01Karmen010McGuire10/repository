DC Standards - Working Group Charter

  • The group shall be a forum for individuals involved in various standardization efforts to keep in touch with each other and to monitor the progress of the Dublin Core as it makes its way through the different standards processes. This group is specifically concerned with issues related to the formalisation of Dublin Core, and its acceptance within standards initiatives such as CEN, ISO, and NISO
