Field labels vs DC Names

  • Examples of the same custom labels for different things:

    • "Event date”
      • in Astronomy database is date of event represented in the original resource
      • in Mount St. Helens it is the date the photo was taken
    • "Publication date”
      • in Astronomy is the date the slide was made available
      • in Mount St. Helens it is the date of scanning.

  • All mapped to DC dates

  • Guideline - choose the label that would be most important to the user of the collection
