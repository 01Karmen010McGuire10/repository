Lessons learned

  • Beware of influence of “legacy” databases on metadata choices

  • Need for constant review and evolution as our understanding or standards change, as new databases are added

  • Need for reengineering when we change our minds or when standards change (flexible software, ability to remap DC, do global updates)

  • Evolving standards, software and paradigms make it essential for the catalogers to talk to the programmers
