Qualifier Recommendation

  • DC.Title.Alternative (NOT Alternate) - will include Abbreviations, as well as Translations (even if not translated on text)

  • DC.Title.Series has not been accepted as the Series Information should be placed in DC.Identifier and identified as a Resource in DC.Type (second level controlled value) (See also notes to Citatation WG)
