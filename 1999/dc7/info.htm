General Information

Accommodation

A certain number of rooms for the workshop participants has been reserved at the following hotel:

    Hotel IBIS Frankfurt Friedensbrücke, Speicherstr. 3-5, D-60327 Frankfurt, phone: +49 69 273030, fax: +49 69 237024.

Contact for your reservation: Mr. Toepper

Please state the following on your booking:

    Keyword: DUBLIN CORE WORKSHOP
    Double / single room
    Smoker / non-smoker
    Arrival / departure dates

In the rooms are plugging facilities for notebooks. A separate meeting room is also reserved for the participants of the Dublin Core Meeting for social gatherings in the evening.

Double or single room: DM 125.00 (incl. VAT), breakfast DM 15.00 (incl. VAT)

Take the train S8 direction "Hanau" to get from the airport to the main railway station.

The hotel is situated near the main railway station and can be reached by train and tram-stop (Baseler Platz), from where it is is a walk of 200 m. Die Deutsche Bibliothek can be reached directly railway station by underground (U 5 direction Preungesheim) within 10 minutes (stop Adickesallee/Deutsche Bibliothek).

Participants must make their individual reservations by October 1st.

Getting to the Deutsche Bibliothek Frankfurt am Main

Frankfurt Airport

Meals

The Workshop fee includes all the meals. Coffee and tea will be served during the breaks. Lunch is offered at noon. In the evenings there will be one dinner in a historical place where Goethe used to stay and one dinner in the restaurant of Die Deutsche Bibliothek.
