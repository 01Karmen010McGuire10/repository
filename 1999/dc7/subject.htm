DC Subject, Description, Language WG Meeting - DC7

Background information

The DC-SubDesc Working Group was set up in early 1999 alongside other WGs covering the various DC elements. The WG's main tasks have been to review the element definitions and to recommend key qualifiers.

The DC-SubDesc Working Group has been fairly quiet, the most lively discussion has been in connection with the proposed "audience" qualifier.
