The 7th Dublin Core Metadata Workshop

October 25-27, 1999
Die Deutsche Bibliothek Frankfurt am Main, Germany
We greatly appreciate the National Science Foundation, the Coalition for Networked Information and the Joint Information Systems Committee sponsoring this workshop.

Die Deutsche Bibliothek and OCLC Online Computer Library Center, Inc. are pleased to host the 7th Dublin Core Metadata Workshop.

This workshop will be the seventh in a series of pivotal events to the development and promotion of the Dublin Core Metadata Initiative. The Dublin Core Metadata Invitational Workshop Series began in March of 1995, convening a broad range of information professionals to address the problem of improving resource discovery on the Internet.

The goal of this seventh workshop will be to consolidate the developments of the various Dublin Core working groups, to share implementation experiences, to promote Dublin Core in supporting interoperability amongst heterogeneous metadata systems.
