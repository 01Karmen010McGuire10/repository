<div class="rss-description"> 
<p>2002-03-12, The joint project of two national libraries: the National library of Russia (St.-Petersburg) and Russian state library (Moscow). The main aim of the register is to provide the verified information sources primarily for specialists in librarianship, information sciences, humanities and social sciences. Database supplied with rubricator that describes subject coverage. Records internal format is Dublin Core. Resources description language is Russian.</p>
