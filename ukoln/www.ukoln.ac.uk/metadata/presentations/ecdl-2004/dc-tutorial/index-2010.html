ECDL 2004 Tutorial: Encoding DC in (X)HTML, XML and RDF

This half-day tutorial was given by Andy Powell during ECDL2004 at the University of Bath in September 2004. It describes the abstract model for DC and how to encode DC metadata in three syntaxes - XHTML, XML and RDF/XML.
