Mailing lists

  • JISC repositories mailing list, jisc-repositories@jiscmail.ac.uk (administered by the Digital Repositories Support team) (http://www.jiscmail.ac.uk/lists/jisc-repositories.html)
"This is an open list for the discussion of any aspect of international repository activity, including repository technology and policy, plus any other repository-related issues. The list is administered by the JISC Digital Repositories Programme support team and is intended for use by anyone working with or interested in repositories."
  • CETIS Metadata and Digital Repositories SIG mailing list, cetis-metadata@jiscmail.ac.uk (http://www.jiscmail.ac.uk/lists/cetis-metadata.html)
"This list is for members of the UK HE/FE community who wish to learn about and evaluate metadata specifications, including the IMS Learning Resource Metadata."
  • CETIS Educational Content SIG mailing list, cetis-ecsig@jiscmail.ac.uk (http://www.jiscmail.ac.uk/lists/cetis-ecsig.html)
"The primary aim of this group is to track, evaluate and support the UK FE/HE sectors' uptake and implementation of standards relating to learning content interoperability, in particular the ims content packaging, content management and learning design specifications."
  • JISC development mailing list, jisc-development@jiscmail.ac.uk (http://www.jiscmail.ac.uk/lists/jisc-development.html)
