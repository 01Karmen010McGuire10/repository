Archived by Tom Baker, 2014-02-03

CEN DC-MMI a working group of the European Committee for Standardisation.  It
active for several years in the early 2000s, until circa 2004.  DCMI
participants included Tom Baker, Makx Dekkers, Rachel Heery, Pete Johnston, and
Robina Clayphan.  The group received some funding to produce "CWAs" (guidelines
and application profiles).

The Web and FTP addresses for DC-MMI materials (e.g., see below) stopped
working soon after cessation of the activity in circa 2005.  The directory
"ftparchive" holds materials snapshotted from the FTP archive around that time.
It appears to hold copies of key deliverables but not of drafts or meeting
minutes.

As of February 2014, CWA 15248 is still available at [1].  Others may still
be available as well.

[1] http://www.cen.eu/cen/Sectors/Sectors/ISSS/CWAdownload/Pages/CWA%2015248.aspx
