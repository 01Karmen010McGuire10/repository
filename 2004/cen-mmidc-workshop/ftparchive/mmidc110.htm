Guidelines for MMI-DC proposals for new work

(CEN/ISSS Workshop on Metadata for Multimedia Information - Dublin Core)

Preparing proposals for future work items is an ongoing process in the MMI-DC workshop. The funding body for MMI-DC is the European Commission as part of eEurope 2005 Action Plan.

Proposed projects must support policy objectives in the eEurope 2005 Action Plan:

  • eBusiness
  • eGovernment
  • eHealth
  • eLearning
  • e-Inclusion

Please look at the action plan attached to this document:

ftp://ftp.cenorm.be/public/ws-mmi-dc/mmidc110_eEurope.pdf

For describing a proposal you must use the Grant Application Form:

ftp://ftp.cenorm.be/public/ws-mmi-dc/mmidc110_GA-form.doc


Although it is possible to make suggestions for a new work item at any time, either at a meeting or as a mail to the MMI-DC mailing list, they can only be submitted in accordance with deadlines laid down by the eEurope process. These deadlines will be announced by a mail from the Workshop Secretariat.

Proposals should be submitted to the Workshop Secretariat who will compile these together for consideration of the workshop members.

Proposals should be based in the success criteria for good proposals given in the attached document and should indicate who the committed partners are and their ability to offer as many voluntary days as they request paid days.
