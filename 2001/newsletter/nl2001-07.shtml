Action!

  • 2001-07-16 DCMI-Libraries Working Group Meeting to be held during IFLA on August 22, 2001

    An official meeting of the Dublin core Metadata Initiative Libraries Working Group (DCMI-Lib WG) will be held during the IFLA meeting on August 22, 2001. This is a DCMI meeting and does not require IFLA registration. All DCMI Libraries working group members and IFLA attendees are invited. Advance registration is requested.
