SC 29/WG 11's Approval By Resolutions 2.8.3 taken at the fifty-third SC 29/WG 11 meeting, 2000-07-17/21, Beijing, China (ref. SC 29 N 3738)
Statement of Expected Benefits and Responsibilities In the interests of improving the interoperability of metadata standards, SC 29/WG 11 would like to establish a liaison. The focus of this would initially be related to our MPEG-7 (Multimedia Content Description) work item. SC 29/WG 11 believes that both parties can benefit from the knowledge and experience of each others' communities and that a liaison will lead to better harmonization of metadata standards.
