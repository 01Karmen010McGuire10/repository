DCMI Working Groups - Qualifier Proposal Information

last updated: 2000-03-31

Introduction

The Dublin Core working groups are in the process of identifying the qualifiers that pertain to their group's focus. This page is intended only as a central linkage point for the discussions that are occuring on the working group's mailing list.

1999-12-10 Final Proposals Due
1999-12-24 Final Set of DC Qualifiers

Checklist for Working Group Chairs for Submission of Qualifier Proposals

1. Working Group defining the qualifier.
2. Which DC element is being qualified?
3. Proposed name of the qualifier.
4. Definition of the qualifier.
5. Does the qualifier refine the semantics of the element? Yes/No
6.

Is the qualifier a controlled vocabulary (enumerated list of values) maintained by the DCMI?

 Yes/No
7.

Is the qualifier a controlled vocabulary (enumerated list of values) maintained by another organization?

If YES, please supply a URL pointing to further information about the vocabulary (if available).

 Yes/No
8.

Is the qualifier a formal encoding format?

If YES, please supply a URL pointing to further information about the format (if available).

 Yes/No
9. Qualifier justification or explanation Optional, but recommended
10. Identify potential overlap/conflicts with other qualifiers, if applicable. Optional
