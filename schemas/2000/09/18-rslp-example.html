RSLP Collection Description XML/RDF Example

Andy Powell, Michael Heaney, and Lorcan Dempsey wrote a very nice article regarding the creation, description and management of collections. The syntactic example they use is slightly problematic from the standpoint of not being XML/RDF complient. Additionally, the example does not incorporate the Dublincore notion of of encoding schemes as modelled by RDF classes (e.g. Data Types) and reflected in the (not yet approved: 20000918) Dublin Core Qualified Schema.

The following document is designed to begin to address what it means to bring this example into RDF and DCQ (as modeled as an RDF Schema) semantic and syntactic complience.... More details on this is required.

Until this detail has been added... the following angley-bracket examples will hopefully illustrate thes issues: The original RDF/XML Example, the example modified and the differencesbetween the original example and updated one.
