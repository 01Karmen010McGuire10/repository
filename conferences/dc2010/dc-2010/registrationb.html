DC-2010

DC-2010 is a 3 day international conference on Dublin Core and Metadata Applications.  It is being held at the Pittsburgh Hilton, in Pittsburgh, PA on October 20-22, 2010 (Wednesday, Thursday and Friday).


 Registration Fees       Early  After 10 Sept
   Full 3 Day Conference (Wednesday, Thursday, Friday)
      General       $620        $840
      Students (with proof)       $310        $420
      
   Daily Rate
      General                    $280
      Students (with proof)                    $140

How to Register


There are 3 ways to register!  Registrations are valid once payment is received and processed.
 
Online:  Online Registration Form
You will receive an automatic confirmation email after you register online.
Once your payment has been processed, you will receive a 2nd email with an attached receipt.
 
By Fax: Download a Registration Form and fax to +001-301-495-0810
Once your payment  has been processed, you will receive an email confirmation with an attached receipt.
 
By Mail: Download a Registration Form and mail to:
          DC-2010
          c/o ASIS&T
          1320 Fenwick Lane, #510
          Silver Spring, Maryland 20910
          USA
Once your payment  has been processed, you will receive an email confirmation with an attached receipt.

Cancellations and Refund Policy

Cancellations and refund requests must be made in writing (e-mail or fax) until 17 September, 2010 (90% of the paid fee will be returned, 10% is retained as cancellation fee).  No cancellations will be accepted after this date.

Students

To qualify for the student rate, you must be a full-time student.  You will be asked to provide the name of your educational institution and a student ID when you check in for the conference.

Dinner DC-2010

1 Dinner ticket (Thursday evening, 21 October) is included with all DC-2010 full 3 day conference registrations .  If you are registering for a single day or would like to bring a guest, tickets can be purchased separately for $60 each. 

Hotel Registration

Attendees must make their own hotel and travel arrangements.  Both DC-2010 and ASIST 2010 will be held at the Pittsburgh Hilton.  Click here for hotel information.  

ASIS&T 2010

The American Society for Information Science and Technology (ASIS&T) annual meeting is being held immediately following DC-2010, at the Pittsburgh Hilton in Pittsburgh on October 22-27, 2010.
