DC-2010

DC-2010 is a 3 day international conference on Dublin Core and Metadata Applications.  It is being held at the Grand Pittsburgh Downtown (formerly the Pittsburgh Hilton), in Pittsburgh, PA on October 20-22, 2010 (Wednesday, Thursday and Friday).

      General                    $280
      Students (with proof)                    $140

How to Register


There are 3 ways to register!  Registrations are valid once payment is received and processed.
 
Online:  Online Registration Form (no longer available)
You will receive an automatic confirmation email after you register online.
Once your payment has been processed, you will receive a 2nd email with an attached receipt.
 
By Fax: Download a Registration Form (no longer available) and fax to +001-301-495-0810
Once your payment  has been processed, you will receive an email confirmation with an attached receipt.
 
By Mail: Download a Registration Form (no longer available) and mail to:
          DC-2010
          c/o ASIS&T
          1320 Fenwick Lane, #510
          Silver Spring, Maryland 20910
          USA
Once your payment  has been processed, you will receive an email confirmation with an attached receipt.

Cancellations and Refund Policy

Cancellations and refund requests must be made in writing (e-mail or fax) until 24 September, 2010 (90% of the paid fee will be returned, 10% is retained as cancellation fee).  No cancellations will be accepted after this date.

Students

To qualify for the student rate, you must be a full-time student.  You will be asked to provide the name of your educational institution and a student ID when you check in for the conference.

Conference Materials 

Please check in at the Dublin Core registration table to pick up your conference materials during the On-Site Registration Hours posted below.  Everyone will receive a name badge which must be worn to every session and event.   

On-Site Registration

On-site registration will be available at the Grand Pittsburgh Downtown (formerly the Pittsburgh Hilton, located at 600 Commonwealth Place, Pittsburgh, PA 15222, Tel: 1-412-560-1751), during the on-site registration hours below.  All speakers and registrants must check-in at the DC-2010 registration area to pick up your name badge and conference materials.

Tuesday, Oct. 19 4-6pm
Wednesday, Oct. 20 7-10am, 1-3pm
Thursday, Oct. 21 7-10am, 1-3pm
Friday, Oct. 22 7-10am, 1-3pm

 

Dinner DC-2010

1 Dinner ticket (Thursday evening, 21 October) is included with all DC-2010 full 3 day conference registrations .  If you are registering for a single day or would like to bring a guest, tickets can be purchased separately for $60 each. 

Hotel Registration

Attendees must make their own hotel and travel arrangements.  Both DC-2010 and ASIST 2010 will be held at the Grand Pittsburgh Downtown (formerly the Pittsburgh Hilton).  Click here for hotel information.  

ASIS&T 2010

The American Society for Information Science and Technology (ASIS&T) annual meeting is being held immediately following DC-2010, at the Pittsburgh Hilton in Pittsburgh on October 22-27, 2010.
