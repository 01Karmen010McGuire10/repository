 		Call for Contributed Papers & Posters

Contributed Papers:

The Conference track will present refereed papers on metadata and related areas of concern including the following:

  • Conceptual models and fundamental principles
  • Globalization and localization
  • Metadata for communities of discourse and practice
  • Enterprise metadata
  • Metadata registries and registry services
  • Interoperability among metadata systems and standards
  • Search engines and metadata
  • Implementation of systems and tools for metadata applications

    All accepted papers will be published online and in print as the DC-2003 Conference Proceedings.

    Posters:

    The DC-2003 Poster Session will provide an opportunity for select presentation in an interactive or graphic format of formative and ongoing research as well as new directions in implementation of metadata systems and services.
