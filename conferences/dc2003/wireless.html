Wireless Laptop Connectivity

Wireless laptop connectivity will be provided throughout the DC-2003 Conference & Workshop. As a result of the availability of wireless, the number of dedicated machines available for checking your email at Bell Harbor will be limited.

Each of the major conference rooms as well as the lobby in the Bell Harbor International Conference Center has its own SSID. See the listing of SSIDs on the right.

Important Note: Please be sure to set your wireless network card to use "Infrastructure Mode".
