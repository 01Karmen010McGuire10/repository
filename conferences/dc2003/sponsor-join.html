DC-2003 Sponsorships Categories

Held in the U.S. for the first time in five years, the Dublin Core Conference & Workshop is the premier conference for discussing metadata use in asset discovery and retrieval, management, preservation and use. Sponsors have a unique opportunity to interact with 225-300 international leaders, including both scholars and practitioners, from the private and public sector who will:

  • share inquiries, methodologies, and results from their latest metadata studies
  • share service and product developments and evaluations
  • explore future directions for metadata use in product and service solutions
