    EIONET - The Network

    EIONET is the main vehicle of the European Environment Agency to collect data, information and knowledge for the process of reporting on the state of environment. The core network connects more than 500 institutions in the 18 EEA member countries.

    Each country operates a co-ordinating institute (National Focal Point - NFP) and nine expertise centres (National Reference Centres - NRCs).
