How to get to Manzanillo
Español / English


Manzanillo, the "Emerald of the Pacific", is located in the bay of the state of Colima, at 1290 kilometres from Mexico City, the capital of the country. In order to reach this land of mafnificient beaches and golden sands, you can choose among the following alternatives:

By air: the Playa de Oro International Airport is situated at 44 kilometres from downtown Manzanillo and 35 kilometres away from the Hotel Zone. It receives flights coming from the United States, Canada and the largest Mexican cities.

Domestic Flights

By land: the trip from Mexico City takes 14 hours, approximately. The buses covering the route depart from the Norte Terminal on Avenida Cien Metros 4907. The vehicles go through Colima, the capital of the state of the same name, before getting to the coast.

In addition, Manzanillo is interconnected with the main western Mexican cities through the system of federal highways.
