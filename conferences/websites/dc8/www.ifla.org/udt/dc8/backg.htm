    During your stay in Ottawa for the DC-8 Conference meals will be provided as follows:
    • all breaks
    • continental breakfast

    Note: On Friday, 6th October only the continental breakfast will be provided.

    Wednesday, 4th October

    Dinner at the National Gallery of Canada

    Recognized the world over for its magnificent collections and intriguing architecture, the Gallery is also Canada's foremost event venue. Few Canadian landmarks will enhance our special event quite like the
    National Gallery.

    Designed by Canadian architect Moshe Safdie, the Gallery is an internationally acclaimed, state-of-the-art museum. We will be dining in "The Great Hall", a shining jewel in the night sky, it brings grandeur and majesty to every event. As magnificent as it is inside, the view outside enchants as well with views of the mighty Ottawa River, lush Gatineau Hills and impressive Parliament Buildings.

    Thursday, 5th October

    Dinner at the Bay Street Bistro

    A California-Italian style Bistro and outdoor cafe. Located at the base of the Main Conference Hotel and walking distance to downtown.
