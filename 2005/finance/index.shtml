DCMI Financial Reports

Annual Audits (2008—   )

The following Annual Audits cover the period between 23 December 2008 and 30 June 2013 when DCMI functioned as DCMI Ltd.—a company limited by guarantee in Singapore. On 30 June 2013, DCMI Ltd. ceased operations in Singapore and became a project of the Association for Information Science and Technology (ASIS&T)—a U.S. 501(c)(3) non-profit. Annual reporting for DCMI starting 1 July 2013 will be part of the overall ASIS&T annual audit.
