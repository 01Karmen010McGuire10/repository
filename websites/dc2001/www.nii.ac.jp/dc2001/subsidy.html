Travel Subsidies for DC-2001

The Dublin Core Metadata Initiative (DCMI) will provide limited travel subsidies for applicants who need assistance to attend DC-2001. Please note that applicants will have to find additional funds from their home institutions or other sources. Please do not apply for a travel subsidy unless participation will be impossible without it. Our funds are limited, and are intended to promote broad distribution of participants.

Priority for travel subsidies will be accorded to:

  1. Individuals who are active in DC working groups
  2. Individuals who will be attending the workshop track as well as the conference and tutorial tracks, and
  3. Individuals associated with, or planning active Dublin Core projects.

Applicants who wish to apply for travel subsidy funds should send a request to:

travel@dublincore.org

Please include your contact information (Name, Address, Telephones, and email address) and a brief paragraph indicating your position with respect to the priority conditions you meet.
