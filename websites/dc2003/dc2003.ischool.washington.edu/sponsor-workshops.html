Sponsor Workshop/Demonstrations

The Sponsor Workshop/Demonstrations will be held daily from 8:30AM to 10:00AM in the Sound, Cove and Marina conference rooms and are free to all DC-2003 full and daily registrants. Room capacity for each 1-1/2 hour Workshop/Demonstration varies with seating configuration. For details, see the room capacity tables below.

A continental breakfast is served to all Workshop/Demonstration participants beginning at 7:45AM. The Workshop/Demonstrations are scheduled opposite the DCMI tutorials in the Bay Auditorium. Approximately 80-85% of Conference participants are available for the Workshop/Demonstration. Promotional materials for the Workshop/Demonstration are distributed to all registrants in the DC-2003 information packets as well as being made available in advance through the DC-2003 Web site.

A 1.5MBPS Internet connection is available is available for the Sound, Cove and Marina conference rooms.
