Usage Board

The Usage Board evaluates proposed vocabulary terms (or changes to existing terms) in light of grammatical principle, semantic clarity, and overlap with existing terms. To proposals that are accepted, it assigns a specific status. The Usage Committee strives for consensus, justifying its decisions and interpretations in terms both of principle and of empirical practice.
