Converting Word documents to Adobe PDF

Microsoft Word includes the ability to convert Word documents to Adobe PDF. The following instructions will help you set up your Conversion Settings to obtain high resolution quality.

Conversion Settings

1 In Microsoft Word, go to the Acrobat -> Change Conversion Settings menu.
2 The Conversion Settings dialog will display.
 
3

Click on the Edit Conversion Settings button. The Job Options dialog will display.

Change the settings on each tab (General, Compression, Fonts, Color, Advanced) as specified in the screens below.
General options
 
Compression options
 
Fonts options
 
Color options
 
Advanced options
 
4 After making the necessary changes, you may save your settings. Click on the Save As button and save your settings as DCMI.joboptions
 
5 After saving your settings, click on the OK button.
6 To convert a Word document to PDF, go to the Acrobat -> Convert to Adobe PDF menu.
