Quotation

HTML Tag:
<Q></Q>
Guidelines:
Mark up quotations. Do not use quotation markup for formatting effects such as indentation.
Warnings:
The Q element is not supported in Navigator (up to version 4.x).
Priority:
[Priority 2] A Web content developer should satisfy this checkpoint.

Browser View:

The preamble to the U.S. Constitution begins, We the People of the United States

Source View:

<P>The preamble to the U.S. Constitution begins, <Q>We the People of the United States</Q></P>
