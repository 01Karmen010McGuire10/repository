Navigating in WinCVS

1

Navigating in WinCVS is very similar to navigating in Windows Explorer.

Double-click on directories to drill down in the file structure.

To move up in the hierarchy, you may select a directory in the left column, or alternatively, click on the Up one level icon (shown below circled in red).
2 Double-click on files to open them in the editor you specified in your WinCVS Preferences. After editing files, you will need to commit them.
You may now add documents.
