Links

"Embedded in HTML" Link Guidelines:
  1. On Working Group pages, the Deliverables table will be collapsed to 3 columns (Scheduled Milestone, Editor/Contact, Status/History) and the document will be linked with embedded links.
  2. Links listed under "History" will be linked with embedded links.
  3. All "mailto" links will be linked with embedded links.
"Visible in WYSIWYG View" Link Guidelines:
  1. In the DCMI head section of all documents, all URLs will be visible in WYSIWYG view.
  2. In the body content area, relative references will be given (the [DCMI] style of references) and in the "References" section, the title of the reference will have an embedded hyperlink and the URL will be visible in WYSIWYG view.
  3. URL's that link to Membership or Mailing list information visible in WYSIWYG view.
