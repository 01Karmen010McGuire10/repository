Moderator:  John A. Kunze (jak@ckm.ucsf.edu)

Proposed "dc-date" charter:

Starting with the work of the DC-5 Date subgroups, finish naming and defining the DC.Date subelements.  Coordinate with the ISO 8601 profile effort so that it reflects range and approximate date requirements agreed on in Helsinki.  Submit finished specs to meta2 and subelements group.
