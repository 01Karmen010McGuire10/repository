Dublin Core Working Groups

DC in Multiple Languages

Moderator:  Thomas Baker (Thomas.Baker@cs.ait.ac.th or Thomas.Baker@gmd.de)

The Dublin Core is short and general enough to be easily implemented in German, Thai, or any other language. This Working Group examines issues related to the definition, maintenance, use, and linkage between these multiple versions.
