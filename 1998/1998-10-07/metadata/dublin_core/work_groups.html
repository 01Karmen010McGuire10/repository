Working Groups emerging from DC-5:

The Helsinki meeting identified a number of areas (some new, some old) which require focused effort for resolution.  Working Groups have formed around these issues. 

The list of groups follows, along with the moderators who have volunteered to shephard the efforts of these working groups and prepare a report on the results.
